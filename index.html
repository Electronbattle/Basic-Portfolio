About Me

I was born right after a meteorite made of the vibration-absorbing mineral vibranium crashed in my home land Wakanda. I sensed early on that outsiders would exploit Wakanda for this valuable resource, so as the ruler, I, King T'Chaka, (my Wakandan name) concealed this country from the outside world.

My first wife N'Yami died while in labor with T'Challa, and my second wife Ramonda was taken prisoner by Anton Pretorius during a visit to her homeland of South Africa, so for most of my son T'Challa was raised by me alone.

My father was murdered by the adventurer Ulysses Klaw in an attempt to seize the vibranium mound. With his people still in danger, a young T'Challa used Klaw's sound weapon on Klaw and his men, shattering Klaw's right hand and forcing him to flee. This made me a proud father. Now I know when I died the new King T'Challa will be a great Ruler and protect our people!
